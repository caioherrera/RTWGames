Games

  • First game

    In the first game, you will play with another user, and we will give you a theme (for example, "epic movies"). Then, you will have 30 seconds to list as many examples related to the theme as you can (for example, if the theme is "epic movies", you can list "Star Wars", "The Lord of the Rings", etc). After both players have finished, you will receive a score based on your list.


  • Second game

    In the second game, you will receive some examples of a certain theme, unknown to you. Your task is to discover, as soon as possible, which is the theme. You will have 30 seconds to do that. After you discover the theme, or the time ends, you will receive your score.


  • Third game

    The third game is played in two parts, and you will play with another user as well. In the first part, each one of you will receive a different example, and you have to describe this example in 3 words or expressions. In the second part of this game, you will receive the expressions the other player used to descibe their theme, and you will have 30 seconds to guess what it was.
